campus life

THE CHOICE SCHOOL- CAMPUS

The Choice School is a private school in Kerala, India, built in 1991. It is owned by The Choice Group under the leadership of Jose Thomas Olassa. The school follows CBSE syllabus. It consists over 2200 students and 250 staff members. The school is attended by both day scholars as well as boarding school students.

SWIMMING POOL

The swimming pool in our school is very spacious and has very clean water. The facilities here are good and privacy is followed. Coaching availabilities are also available. Safety and security is also followed.

LIBRARY

The library here has so many books you can choose from. If you ever feel like you want a calm, peace of mind, you can go to the Choice School's library and find the book of your choice. There are not only books but newspapers and magazines, so if you feel like you want to know about what is going on around the world or want to get to know some pretty cool facts the library is the best place to go.

school achievements

CBSE National Badminton Championship 2019
Won first prize in CBSE National badminton champioship

The Choice School bagged the first place at the National Badminton tournament in the under 19 category for boys held at Varanasi from 3rd to 7th of December 2019. Winners from all CBSE clusters including National and international CBSE schools participated in the event.

Under 11 and Under 13 Boy's Badminton Tournament
Won first prize in under 11 and 13 boy's badminton tournament

Our Under 11 and Under 13 boy's badminton teams participated in the Badminton tournament conducted by Riverbourne Club, Tripunithura and secured first place in both the categories.

Young Entrepreneurs Summit
Won first place in Young Entrepreneurs's Summit

Young Entrepreneurs’ Summit (YES) , at Kodaikanal International School. The Choice team bags the 1st place for the “Business Pitch” where they presented a solar fryer with all necessary business credentials.
